Yves Hoebeke is the founder of Bytesupply, LLC.

I graduated from the Royal Atheneaum in Ghent Belgium with a degree in Humanities and Science while simultaneously finishing my course in computer programming with IBM Belgium.

And so a software engineering career was started. It encompassed military applications, implement image digitalization on mainframe adjuncts for an oil conglomerate, accounting and medical billing on mini-frames to today's technologies addressing a variety of solutions.

I brought this company back to life (see History) to have hands-on involvement in the creation, including team building and management; development, implementation and maintenance of solutions needed by my customers.

Server side software engineering in Go is my passion.

I am a solution looking for a problem.
